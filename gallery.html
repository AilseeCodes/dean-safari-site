Web Design & Development

We thank Ailsa Watson for the design and development of this digital platform. Built entirely from the ground up, this site leverages a secure, high-performance architecture utilizing Google Antigravity and Firebase hosting. Throughout this project, Ailsa managed the full initial infrastructure configuration and security-conscious backend deployment to the final interface design and project handover.

This project was developed in close collaboration with business owner Justine McGregor, whose input was instrumental in refining the platform's content and user experience.

Ailsa Watson is a Zoologist with a postgraduate background in Applied Conservation Science and African Ecosystems Management, as well as a qualified FGASA Level 2 Field Guide. You can learn more about her professional background and portfolio at LinkedIn Profile.
