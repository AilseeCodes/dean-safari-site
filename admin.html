Privacy & Data Protection Policy

Zimbabwean Data Protection Guidelines

Under Zimbabwe's Cyber Security and Data Protection Act [Chapter 12:07], organizations must implement Privacy by Design. As a safari operator collecting guest details (such as names, passport details, and health constraints), you should review and update this policy annually or whenever your operational storage practices change.

Key Regulatory Resource: You can check official compliance directives and updates on the POTRAZ Website (potraz.gov.zw)—Zimbabwe's designated Data Protection Authority.
